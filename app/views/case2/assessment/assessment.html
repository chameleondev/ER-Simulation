Risk assessment: PE severity

Simplified PESI score (sPESI)
Parameter Points
Age 1 (if age >80 years)
Cancer 1
Chronic heart failure or chronic pulmonary disease 1
Pulse rate ≥110 bpm 1
Systolic blood pressure <100 mm Hg 1
Arterial oxyhaemoglobin saturation <90% 1

0 points = 30-day mortality risk 1.0% (95% CI 0.0–2.1%)
≥1 point = 30-day mortality rate 10.9% (95% CI 8.5–13.2%)

ESC 2014 PE guideline classification:

  • sPESI=0 (PESI class I–II): Low risk
  • sPESI≥1 (PESI class III–IV): Intermediate risk
    • RV enlargement AND positive lab markers (MI or heart failure): Intermediate-high risk
    • RV enlargement OR positive lab markers OR both tests negative: Intermediate-low ris
  • Shocked/hypotensive patient: High risk
