  • D-dimer: a negative result excludes PE/DVT in patients at lower clinical risk. D-dimer may be elevated in many conditions other than PE/DVT 3,4
  • Troponin: indicates myocardial necrosis and may be elevated in acute MI or higher risk PE 3,4
  • Biochemistry: renal function and electrolytes (raised creatinine in renal failure); LFTs (impaired liver function and associated low albumin); glucose (infection associated with diabetes); TFTs (hypothyroidism) 3,4
