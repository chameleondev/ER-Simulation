Follow-up and patient education

  • Before the patient is discharged, advice should be given, including 1:
    • Current dosing, frequency, duration and any dose changes needed
    • If using rivaroxaban, tablets should be taken with food
    • Signs and symptoms of recurrent VTE and bleeding events
    • When to seek medical advice (provide telephone number or contact details)
  • Notify the patient’s primary care team of the diagnosis and treatment
  • Organize and notify patient of follow-up appointment (e.g. in 21 days’ time if using rivaroxaban) – patient education will be repeated at this time
  • Treatment review every 3 months
  • Consultant appointment booked for 6 weeks to 3 months’ time
