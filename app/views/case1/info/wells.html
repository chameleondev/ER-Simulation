• Score 1 point each for the following:
    • Active cancer
    • Paralysis, paresis or plaster cast
    • Bedridden for 3+ days or major surgery within 12 weeks
    • Pain/tenderness along deep vein system
    • Swollen leg
    • Calf swelling >3 cm more than asymptomatic leg
    • Pitting oedema in symptomatic leg only
    • Collateral superficial veins
    • History of DVT
  • Subtract 2 points if an alternative cause is considered at least as likely as DVT
  • DVT is considered:
    • Likely if score is ≥2
    • Unlikely if score is ≤1 (consider trauma, cellulitis)
