Emergency Room
Simulation Training

Using three patient cases, this training simulation is designed for junior healthcare professionals in emergency care to support the prompt diagnosis and treatment of thromboembolic disorders.

Working through the patient simulations, guided by helpful hints, consultant advice and learning materials, will help you to apply scientific knowledge to clinical practice. Decision points indicate the key stages of diagnosis and assessment and provide an opportunity to evaluate your level of understanding in each case.

  • Case 1
    34-year old male presents with pain and
    swelling in the left leg
  • Case 2
    42-year old female with acute breathlessness
